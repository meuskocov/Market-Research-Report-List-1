<p><ul><li>Inline</li><li>Clamp-On</li><li>Insertion</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The supersonic flowmeter market encompasses three primary types: inline, clamp-on, and insertion flowmeters. Inline flowmeters are integrated directly within the pipe, providing accurate measurements unaffected by external factors. Clamp-on flowmeters are mounted externally, using ultrasonic signals to measure flow without disrupting the system. Insertion flowmeters partially enter the flow stream, offering a balance between measurement accuracy and installation ease. Each type caters to specific application needs, balancing factors such as installation convenience, precision, and operational disruption.</p></p>
