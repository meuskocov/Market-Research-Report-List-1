<p><p>The synthetic human secretin market serves two primary applications: exocrine pancreas function testing and Zollinger-Ellison syndrome testing. In exocrine pancreas function testing, synthetic secretin is used to stimulate the pancreas to assess its ability to produce digestive enzymes, aiding in diagnosing conditions like pancreatitis or cystic fibrosis. For Zollinger-Ellison syndrome testing, synthetic secretin helps evaluate acid secretion and identify gastrin-secreting tumors. Both markets are essential for clinical diagnostics, enhancing patient care and treatment effectiveness.</p></p>
