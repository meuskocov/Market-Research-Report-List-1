<p><ul><li>Ion-Exchange HPLC</li><li>Immunoassay</li><li>Boronate Affinity Chromatography</li><li>Direct Enzymatic Assay</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Hemoglobin A1c testing devices market encompasses various technologies for measuring glycemic control in diabetes. Ion-Exchange HPLC separates different hemoglobin forms for accurate results. Immunoassay utilizes antibodies to measure glycated hemoglobin levels, providing quick results. Boronate Affinity Chromatography selectively binds to glucose-modified hemoglobin, offering high specificity. Direct Enzymatic Assays use enzymatic reactions to quantify HbA1c levels, enabling rapid and straightforward testing. Each method caters to specific laboratory needs and enhances diabetes management efficacy.</p></p>
