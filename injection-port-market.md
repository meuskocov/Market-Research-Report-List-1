<p><strong>The Injection Port Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>I-Port Type</li><li>Insuflon Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The injection port market can be categorized into two main types: I-Port and Insuflon. The I-Port type is designed for easy access to subcutaneous tissue, allowing for repeated injections without the need for additional skin penetration, enhancing patient comfort. In contrast, the Insuflon type features a specialized catheter system that provides continuous subcutaneous access for insulin delivery or other medications, promoting better management of chronic conditions. Both types improve convenience and reduce the risk of complications during treatment.</p></p>
