<p><p>The Reduction (NSR) Catalyst Market encompasses two main types: Redox Catalysts and Selective Reduction Catalysts. Redox Catalysts facilitate oxidation-reduction reactions, enhancing chemical processes by transferring electrons between reactants. In contrast, Selective Reduction Catalysts focus on selectively reducing specific compounds, often in pollutant removal applications like NOx reduction in automotive exhaust systems. Both types are crucial in various industrial processes, contributing to improved efficiency, reduced emissions, and sustainable practices in chemical manufacturing and environmental protection.</p></p>
