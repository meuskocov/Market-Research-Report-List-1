<p><strong>The LED Packaging Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>SMD Packaging</li><li>COB Packaging</li><li>CSP Packaging</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The LED packaging market encompasses various types, including SMD (Surface-Mount Device) packaging, COB (Chip on Board) packaging, and CSP (Chip Scale Package) packaging. SMD packaging is widely used for its ease of integration into circuit boards, offering a compact design. COB packaging involves directly mounting LED chips onto a substrate, enhancing thermal management and light efficiency. CSP packaging minimizes the package size by closely integrating the LED chip with the circuit, allowing for higher performance in limited spaces. These types cater to diverse applications across the lighting and display industries.</p></p>
