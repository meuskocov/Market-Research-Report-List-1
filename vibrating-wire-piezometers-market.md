<p><p>Vibrating wire piezometers are instruments used for measuring pore water pressure in soil and rock. They come in two main types: normal and smart. Normal piezometers use a vibrating wire sensor that responds to changes in pressure, providing reliable data for monitoring geotechnical projects. Smart piezometers incorporate advanced technology, such as digital output and wireless communication, allowing for real-time data transmission and remote monitoring, which enhances data accessibility and analysis for improved decision-making in engineering applications.</p></p>
