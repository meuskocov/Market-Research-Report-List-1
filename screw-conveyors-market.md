<p><strong>The Screw Conveyors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Horizontal Installation Screw Conveyors</li><li>Inclined Installation Screw Conveyors</li><li>Vertical Installation Screw Conveyors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Screw conveyors are categorized based on their installation orientation. Horizontal installation screw conveyors are designed to transport bulk materials over short to medium distances along a level plane. Inclined installation screw conveyors, on the other hand, facilitate the movement of materials at an angle, making them suitable for elevating ingredients vertically or diagonally. Vertical installation screw conveyors operate in a vertical arrangement, effectively transporting materials from lower to higher elevations with minimal spillage, ideal for constrained spaces.</p></p>
