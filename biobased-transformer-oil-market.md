<p><ul><li>Natural Ester Transformer Fluid</li><li>Synthetic Ester Transformer Oil</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The biobased transformer oil market primarily includes two types: natural ester transformer fluid and synthetic ester transformer oil. Natural ester fluids are derived from renewable plant materials, offering excellent insulation properties and biodegradability, making them environmentally friendly. On the other hand, synthetic ester transformers are produced from chemical processes but still utilize sustainable resources, providing improved thermal stability and enhanced performance in high-temperature environments. Both types contribute to safer and more sustainable electricity transmission and distribution.</p></p>
