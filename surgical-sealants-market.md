<p><p>The surgical sealants market can be categorized into three main types: natural/biological sealants, synthetic sealants, and semi-synthetic sealants. Natural sealants are derived from biological sources, often providing biocompatibility and promoting healing. Synthetic sealants are chemically manufactured, offering durability and versatility for various applications in surgery. Semi-synthetic sealants combine natural and synthetic components to enhance performance. Each type serves unique purposes in sealing tissues, reducing blood loss, and preventing leaks during surgical procedures, catering to diverse medical needs.</p></p>
