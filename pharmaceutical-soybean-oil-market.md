In contrast, soybean oil for oral use is employed in various formulations, including dietary supplements and capsules, serving as a source of essential fatty acids and enhancing the bioavailability of fat-soluble vitamins. Both types are crucial in maximizing therapeutic effectiveness.</p></p>
