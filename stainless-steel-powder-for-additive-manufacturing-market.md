<p><p>The stainless steel powder for additive manufacturing market is categorized into three main types: austenitic, martensitic, and ferritic grades. Austenitic grades, known for their excellent ductility and corrosion resistance, are ideal for complex geometries. Martensitic grades offer higher strength and hardness, making them suitable for applications requiring durability. Ferritic grades, while having lower corrosion resistance than austenitic types, provide good formability and are cost-effective for specific engineering applications. Each grade serves distinct purposes, driven by their unique mechanical properties.</p></p>
