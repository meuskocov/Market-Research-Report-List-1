<p><p>Pellistor bead chemical sensors are primarily categorized into three market types: electrochemical, optical, and catalytic bead sensors. Electrochemical sensors detect gas concentrations through chemical reactions, offering high sensitivity and specificity. Optical sensors utilize light absorption or scattering to identify gases, providing fast response times. Catalytic bead sensors, like pellistors, work by oxidizing combustible gases, causing a temperature change that is measured to determine gas presence and concentration. Each type serves distinct applications in environmental monitoring and industrial safety.</p></p>
