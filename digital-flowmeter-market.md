<p><ul><li>Field Mounting</li><li>Panel Mounting</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Digital Flowmeter market comprises two primary types: field mounting and panel mounting. Field mounting flowmeters are designed for installation directly at the measurement site, offering ease of access and robust performance in harsh environments. They are often used in industrial settings for real-time monitoring. In contrast, panel mounting flowmeters are integrated into control panels or local displays, providing centralized monitoring and control capabilities. Both types cater to various applications, enhancing efficiency and accuracy in fluid measurement.</p></p>
