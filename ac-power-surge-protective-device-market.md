<p><strong>The AC Power Surge Protective Device Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Common Mode Protection</li><li>Differential Mode Protection</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The AC Power Surge Protective Device (SPD) market encompasses two main types: common mode protection and differential mode protection. Common mode protection addresses surges that occur between ground and the line, safeguarding equipment from voltage spikes that can enter through power cords or signal lines. Differential mode protection, on the other hand, targets surges between the live and neutral wires, ensuring that equipment remains safe from voltage differences that can arise during electrical disturbances. Both types are essential for enhancing equipment reliability and extending lifespan.</p></p>
