<p><p>The isolator valves market includes several types: </p><p>1. **Ball Valves** offer quick shut-off and low pressure drops, making them ideal for on/off applications. </p><p>2. **Butterfly Valves** provide compact, lightweight designs suitable for regulating flow in large pipes. </p><p>3. **Gate Valves** excel at full flow applications, minimizing pressure loss when fully open. </p><p>4. **Globe Valves** are designed for precise flow control, suitable for throttling. </p><p>5. **Other** includes specialized valves like check valves, used for various isolation and flow management needs.</p></p>
