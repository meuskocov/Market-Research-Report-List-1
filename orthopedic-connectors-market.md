<p><strong>The Orthopedic Connectors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Non-Absorbable</li><li>Absorbable</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The orthopedic connectors market is divided into two main types: non-absorbable and absorbable connectors. Non-absorbable connectors, typically made from materials like polypropylene or nylon, are designed to provide long-lasting support, remaining permanently in the body. In contrast, absorbable connectors are made from materials that gradually break down over time, offering temporary support during the healing process before being naturally absorbed by the body. Each type serves distinct surgical needs based on patient recovery and the nature of the orthopedic procedures.</p></p>
