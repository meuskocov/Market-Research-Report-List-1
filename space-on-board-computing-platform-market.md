<p><p>The Space On-board Computing Platform market is categorized into three main types based on orbit types. Low Earth Orbit (LEO) focuses on satellites that operate at altitudes of 160 to 2,000 kilometers, enabling rapid communication and data transfer. Medium Earth Orbit (MEO) refers to satellites positioned between 2,000 and 35,786 kilometers, commonly used for navigation systems. Geostationary Earth Orbit (GEO) involves satellites at approximately 35,786 kilometers, maintaining a fixed position relative to the Earth, ideal for communication and broadcasting services.</p></p>
