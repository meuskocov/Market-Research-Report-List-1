<p><p>The graphite heat exchanger market consists of three primary types: shell and tube, block, and plate heat exchangers. Shell and tube designs feature multiple tubes enclosed in a shell, ideal for high-pressure applications. Block heat exchangers offer a compact structure with interconnected channels, ensuring efficient heat transfer in limited spaces. Plate heat exchangers consist of thin plates stacked to maximize surface area, providing high efficiency and easy maintenance. Each type caters to specific industrial needs, optimizing performance and longevity.</p></p>
