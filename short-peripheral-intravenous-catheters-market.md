<p><p>The Short Peripheral Intravenous Catheters (PIVC) market is categorized into ported and non-ported types. Ported Short PIVCs feature an additional access port, allowing for multiple infusions without the need for catheter replacement, enhancing convenience and reducing patient discomfort. Non-ported Short PIVCs, on the other hand, do not have this access point and are typically used for single infusions. Each type plays a crucial role in clinical settings, catering to different patient needs and treatment protocols.</p></p>
