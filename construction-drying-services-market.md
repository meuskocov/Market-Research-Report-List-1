<p><strong>The Construction Drying Services Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Dehumidification</li><li>Desiccant Drying</li><li>Structural Drying</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Construction drying services encompass various techniques used to remove moisture from structures during or after construction processes. Dehumidification involves using machines to extract moisture from the air, aiding in faster drying of materials. Desiccant drying employs chemical substances that absorb moisture, effective in low-temperature environments. Structural drying focuses on drying out the building materials themselves, such as wood and drywall. Other methods might include air movement and heating solutions, all aimed at preventing mold growth and maintaining structural integrity.</p></p>
