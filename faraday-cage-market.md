<p><p>Faraday cages are protective enclosures that block electromagnetic fields, and their market can be categorized into two main types: metal and benign conductors. Metal Faraday cages utilize materials like aluminum or copper to create strong shielding effects, ideal for sensitive electronics. In contrast, benign conductors, such as specially coated fabrics or plastics, offer lighter, more flexible options, suitable for consumer applications like portable devices. Both types serve critical roles in protecting against interference and maintaining signal integrity in various environments.</p></p>
