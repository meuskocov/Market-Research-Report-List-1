<p><p>The constipation treatment drug market includes various types of medications designed to alleviate symptoms. Laxatives facilitate bowel movements by softening stool or stimulating intestinal activity. Chloride channel activators enhance fluid secretion in the intestines, promoting bowel transit. 5-HT4 receptor agonists stimulate serotonin receptors, increasing gastric motility. GC-C agonists increase intestinal fluid secretion and improve peristalsis. Peripherally acting mu-opioid receptor antagonists counteract opioid-induced constipation by blocking opioid effects in the gut, facilitating bowel movements without affecting pain relief.</p></p>
