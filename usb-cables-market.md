<p><p>The USB cable market is categorized into several types based on data transfer speeds and functionalities. USB 1.1 supports low-speed devices at 1.5 Mbps and full-speed at 12 Mbps. USB 2.0 introduced high-speed capabilities, reaching up to 480 Mbps. USB 3.0 significantly improved performance with SuperSpeed transfer rates of up to 5 Gbps, while USB 3.1 further enhanced this with SuperSpeed+ speeds of 10 Gbps. Each type offers compatibility with a range of devices, catering to various user needs.</p></p>
