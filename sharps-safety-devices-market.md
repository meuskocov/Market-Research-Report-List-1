<p><p>The Sharps Safety Devices market is primarily categorized into two types: Retractable Safety and Passive Safety devices. Retractable Safety devices feature mechanisms that automatically withdraw sharp needles or blades after use, minimizing exposure and risk of injuries. Conversely, Passive Safety devices are designed to protect users without requiring any action before or after use, inherently reducing the likelihood of accidental needle sticks. Both types aim to improve safety and compliance in healthcare settings by reducing sharps-related injuries.</p></p>
