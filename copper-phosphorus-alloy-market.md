<p><p>Copper Phosphorus (CuP) alloys, such as CuP8, CuP10, and CuP15, are widely used in brazing and deoxidizing applications due to their self-fluxing properties and high strength. CuP8 contains 8% phosphorus, offering excellent flow and joint strength, ideal for copper-to-copper brazing. CuP10, with 10% phosphorus, provides enhanced fluidity and corrosion resistance, suitable for HVAC and refrigeration systems. CuP15, with 15% phosphorus, is used for specialized applications requiring higher phosphorus content. Other variants cater to niche industrial needs, offering tailored properties for specific brazing and metallurgical requirements.</p></p>
