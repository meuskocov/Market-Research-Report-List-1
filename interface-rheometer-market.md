<p><strong>The Interface Rheometer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Cone Plate Rheometer</li><li>Concentric Cylinder Rheometer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The interface rheometer market encompasses various types of devices designed to measure the flow and deformation behavior of materials. Cone plate rheometers feature a cone-shaped spindle that rotates over a flat plate, providing precise viscosity measurements under controlled shear rates. In contrast, concentric cylinder rheometers consist of two cylinders, one inside the other, allowing for the assessment of material properties across a range of shear rates. Both types are essential for characterizing complex fluids in industries like food, cosmetics, and polymers.</p></p>
