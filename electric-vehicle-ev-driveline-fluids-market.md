<p><p>The Electric Vehicle (EV) driveline fluids market primarily comprises synthetic oils and mineral oils. Synthetic oils are engineered to provide superior lubrication, enhancing efficiency and extending component life in EV drivetrains. They offer better thermal stability and low-temperature performance, crucial for electric powertrains. On the other hand, mineral oils, derived from refining crude oil, are generally less expensive but may not provide the same level of performance as synthetic options. The choice between these fluids impacts vehicle efficiency, longevity, and maintenance needs.</p></p>
