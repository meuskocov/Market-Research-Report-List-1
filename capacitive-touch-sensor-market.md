<p><strong>The Capacitive Touch Sensor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Surface Capacitive Sensing</li><li>Projected Capacitive Sensing</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The capacitive touch sensor market is primarily divided into two types: surface capacitive sensing and projected capacitive sensing. Surface capacitive sensing detects touch based on changes in capacitance across the surface of a conductive layer, primarily allowing for single-point touch detection. Projected capacitive sensing, on the other hand, utilizes an array of capacitive sensors beneath a protective layer, enabling multi-touch capabilities and improved sensitivity. This technology is widely used in smartphones, tablets, and interactive displays, enhancing user experience.</p></p>
