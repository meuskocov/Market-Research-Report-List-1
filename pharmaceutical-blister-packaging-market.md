<p><p>The pharmaceutical blister packaging market includes several types, each serving unique functions. PVC (polyvinyl chloride) is widely used for its excellent moisture barrier properties, making it suitable for various medications. Lidding foils provide a hermetic seal, ensuring product integrity. Cold form blister packaging offers enhanced protection against environmental factors, ideal for sensitive drugs. PVDC (polyvinylidene chloride) is known for its superior barrier properties against gases and moisture, commonly used in high-quality packaging applications. These types cater to diverse pharmaceutical needs.</p></p>
