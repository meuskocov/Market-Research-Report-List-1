<p><p>The compact wheeled loader market is segmented into three main drive types: hydrodynamic, mechanical, and electrical. Hydrodynamic drive systems use hydraulic fluid to transmit power, providing smooth operation and high torque. Mechanical drive systems utilize gears and components for direct power transfer, offering simplicity and durability. Electrical drive systems rely on electric motors, delivering zero emissions and lower operating costs while enhancing control and energy efficiency. Each type caters to specific operational needs and environmental considerations in various industries.</p></p>
