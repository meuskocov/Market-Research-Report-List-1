<p><p>The utility boats market can be segmented into two primary types based on the motor configuration: inboard motor and outboard motor boats. Inboard motor boats have their engines housed inside the vessel, providing better weight distribution and reduced noise, making them suitable for larger vessels and specific applications like towing. Conversely, outboard motor boats feature engines mounted externally, offering ease of maintenance, portability, and enhanced maneuverability. Each type caters to different user needs, from recreational purposes to commercial and service applications.</p></p>
