Medium denim, weighing around 10-12 ounces, offers a balance of durability and softness, often used in everyday jeans and jackets. Heavy denim, exceeding 12 ounces, is known for its ruggedness and strength, making it ideal for workwear and premium products. Each type caters to different consumer preferences and usage scenarios.</p></p>
