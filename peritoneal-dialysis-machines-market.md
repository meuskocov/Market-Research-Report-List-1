<p><strong>The Peritoneal Dialysis Machines Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Power Type</li><li>Gravity Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Peritoneal dialysis machines market comprises two primary types: power type and gravity type. Power type machines utilize electrical pumps to facilitate the precise flow of dialysate into the peritoneal cavity, ensuring effective filtration and minimal manual intervention. In contrast, gravity type machines rely on gravitational force to fill and drain the dialysate, making them simpler and often more cost-effective. These distinctions cater to varying patient needs, preferences, and healthcare settings within the evolving landscape of renal care.</p></p>
