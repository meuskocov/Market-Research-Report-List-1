<p><p>The split AC market encompasses various designs tailored for different needs. Wall-mounted units are popular for residential spaces due to their efficient cooling and compact size. Floor-standing types offer flexibility in larger areas, providing powerful cooling from ground level. Cassette types are installed in the ceiling, ideal for commercial settings, offering an unobtrusive appearance and even airflow. Ceiling suspended units are similar but designed to hang from ceilings, suitable for high-ceiling spaces. Ducted systems provide centralized cooling, distributing air through ducts for larger buildings.</p></p>
