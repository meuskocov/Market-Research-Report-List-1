<p><p>Industrial monitoring relays encompass various types designed to ensure equipment safety and efficiency. Voltage monitoring relays detect abnormal voltage levels, protecting systems from overvoltage and undervoltage conditions. Level monitoring relays gauge liquid or material levels in tanks, preventing overflow or dry running. Current monitoring relays measure electrical current to safeguard against overloads and ensure optimal operation. Together, these relays enhance operational reliability and minimize downtime, making them essential components in industrial automation and process control.</p></p>
