<p><strong>The Fixed-focus Lenses Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Fixed Iris</li><li>Manual Iris</li><li>Auto Iris</li></ul></p>
<p><p>In the fixed-focus lens market, lenses can be categorized into three types based on their iris mechanisms. Fixed iris lenses offer a constant aperture, providing consistent exposure but limited flexibility in varying light conditions. Manual iris lenses enable users to adjust the aperture manually, allowing for greater control over exposure and depth of field. Auto iris lenses automatically adjust the aperture based on lighting, ensuring optimal image quality in dynamic environments, making them ideal for surveillance and broadcasting applications.</p></p>
