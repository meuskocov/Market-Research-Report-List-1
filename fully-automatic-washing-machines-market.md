<p><strong>The Fully Automatic Washing Machines Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Drum Type</li><li>Pulsator Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The fully automatic washing machine market is primarily segmented into two types: drum type and pulsator type. Drum-type machines utilize a cylindrical drum that rotates to effectively wash and dry clothes, providing gentler care for fabrics and better cleaning efficiency. In contrast, pulsator-type machines employ a rotating disc at the bottom, creating turbulence in water to clean clothes, often being more cost-effective and compact. Both types cater to varying consumer needs, preferences, and budgets, influencing market dynamics.</p></p>
