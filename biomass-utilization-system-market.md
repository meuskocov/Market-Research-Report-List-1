<p><p>The Biomass Utilization System market encompasses various methods for converting organic materials into energy. Direct combustion involves burning biomass for heat or electricity. Anaerobic digestion breaks down organic matter to produce biogas and digestate. Fermentation converts sugars in biomass into bioethanol. Oil extraction focuses on obtaining oils from plant materials for biodiesel production. Pyrolysis thermally decomposes biomass in the absence of oxygen to generate bio-oil, char, and syngas, while gasification transforms biomass into syngas through partial oxidation.</p></p>
