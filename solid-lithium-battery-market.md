<p><p>The solid lithium battery market is primarily divided into two types: polymer-based solid-state lithium batteries and solid-state lithium batteries with inorganic solid electrolytes. Polymer-based batteries utilize polymer materials for their electrolytes, offering flexibility and potentially enhanced safety features. In contrast, inorganic solid electrolyte batteries use crystalline or glass-like materials, which provide high conductivity and stability but may pose manufacturing challenges. Both types aim to improve energy density, safety, and overall performance compared to traditional lithium-ion batteries.</p></p>
