<p><p>The mechanical keyboard shaft market features two primary types: linear and tactile shaft types. Linear shaft types offer a smooth keypress without any tactile feedback, making them ideal for fast typing and gaming due to their consistent resistance. In contrast, tactile shaft types provide noticeable feedback at the actuation point, allowing users to feel when a key is registered, enhancing typing accuracy and comfort. Each shaft type caters to different user preferences, influencing their popularity in gaming and professional environments.</p></p>
