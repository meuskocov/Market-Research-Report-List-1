<p><p>The special animal feed market comprises three primary types: compound feed, fodder, and forage. Compound feed is a balanced mixture of ingredients formulated to meet specific nutritional needs for livestock, promoting growth and productivity. Fodder refers to cultivated food sources, such as grains and legumes, designed to be harvested and fed fresh or dried. Forage includes natural plant materials, like grasses and legumes, that animals graze on, providing essential nutrients while maintaining their health and productivity.</p></p>
