<p><ul><li>Dedicated Short-range Communication</li><li>Mesh</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Wireless communication technology for vehicles encompasses various types, including Dedicated Short-Range Communication (DSRC) and mesh networks. DSRC enables low-latency, high-speed communication between vehicles and infrastructure over short distances, enhancing safety and traffic efficiency. In contrast, mesh networks allow vehicles to communicate with each other and nearby devices without a fixed infrastructure, creating a self-forming and self-healing network. Together, these technologies facilitate real-time data exchange, improving navigation, collision avoidance, and overall driving experience.</p></p>
