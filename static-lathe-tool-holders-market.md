<p><p>Static lathe tool holders are essential components in machining operations, designed to securely hold cutting tools for precision work. The BMT (Bellows Mount Tool) type features a compact design with a bellows mechanism that provides excellent vibration-dampening properties, enhancing tool performance. In contrast, the VDI (Verein Deutscher Ingenieure) tool holder is known for its standardized dimensions and versatility, allowing quick tool changes and adaptability across various machines. Both types cater to specific machining needs, optimizing efficiency and precision.</p></p>
