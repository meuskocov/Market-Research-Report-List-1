<p><p>The Ultrasonic Distance Sensor Module market is primarily divided into two types: Analog and Digital. Analog type sensors output a continuous voltage signal proportional to the measured distance, making them suitable for applications requiring smooth variation in readings. In contrast, Digital type sensors provide discrete data, often via pulse-width modulation or digital signals, facilitating easier integration with microcontrollers. Both types cater to various industries, including robotics, automotive, and automation, addressing specific needs for distance measurement and object detection.</p></p>
