<p><ul><li>Static Seal</li><li>Dynamic Seal</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Ultra High Pressure Seals market is divided into two main types: static seals and dynamic seals. Static seals are designed to create a leak-proof barrier in stationary applications, preventing fluid or gas leakage between fixed components. In contrast, dynamic seals facilitate movement between two surfaces, accommodating changes in position while maintaining a seal under high pressure. Both types are critical for industries such as oil and gas, aerospace, and manufacturing, where reliability and performance under extreme conditions are essential.</p></p>
