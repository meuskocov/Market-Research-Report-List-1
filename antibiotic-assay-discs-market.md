<p><p>Antibiotic assay discs are small paper discs that are impregnated with specific antibiotics and are used in microbiological laboratories to determine the susceptibility of bacterial strains to various antibiotics. These discs are placed on agar plates that have been inoculated with bacteria, and through a process known as disk diffusion, the effectiveness of the antibiotic is assessed by measuring the zone of inhibition around the disc. This method is vital for guiding effective treatment options and combating antibiotic resistance.</p><p>The Antibiotic Assay Discs Market is experiencing significant growth driven by increasing antibiotic resistance, rising prevalence of infectious diseases, and the need for effective antibiotic stewardship programs. Advances in laboratory technology and growing investments in research are also contributing to market expansion. Furthermore, the rising focus on personalized medicine promotes the development of tailored antibiotic therapies, which in turn supports demand for antibiotic susceptibility testing.</p><p>The Antibiotic Assay Discs Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 8.40% during the forecast period.
