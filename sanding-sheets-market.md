<p><p>The sanding sheets market includes dry and wet sanding sheets, each designed for specific applications. Dry sanding sheets are used for general-purpose sanding on wood, metal, or plastic, offering efficient material removal and smooth finishes without water. Wet sanding sheets are used with water or lubricants to reduce dust, prevent clogging, and achieve ultra-smooth finishes, often on surfaces like automotive paint or fine woodworking. Both types vary in grit sizes and materials to suit different tasks and surface requirements.</p></p>
