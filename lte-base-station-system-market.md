<p><strong>The LTE Base Station System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>TDD-LTE</li><li>FDD-LTE</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The LTE base station system market encompasses two primary types: TDD-LTE and FDD-LTE. TDD-LTE (Time Division Duplex) utilizes time slots for upstream and downstream communications, making it efficient for asymmetrical data traffic and ideal for urban environments with variable demand. In contrast, FDD-LTE (Frequency Division Duplex) uses separate frequency bands for uplink and downlink, providing consistent and predictable performance. This separation allows for simultaneous transmission, making FDD-LTE favorable for applications requiring continuous data flow, such as video streaming.</p></p>
