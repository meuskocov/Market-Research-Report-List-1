<p><p>The Consumer Wi-Fi Router market primarily consists of two types: single routers and router packs. Single routers are standalone devices designed to provide wireless connectivity for homes, suitable for smaller spaces with moderate internet usage. In contrast, router packs, often encompassing multiple units, are designed to cover larger areas and provide seamless connectivity through a mesh network. These packs are ideal for larger homes or multi-story buildings, offering enhanced coverage and performance by distributing the signal across several devices.</p></p>
