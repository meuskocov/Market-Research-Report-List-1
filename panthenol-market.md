<p><strong>The Panthenol Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>D-Panthenol</li><li>DL-Panthenol</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Panthenol market primarily consists of two types: D-Panthenol and DL-Panthenol. D-Panthenol, the biologically active form, is widely used in cosmetics, pharmaceuticals, and personal care products for its moisturizing, soothing, and skin-repairing properties. DL-Panthenol, a racemic mixture of D and L forms, is less biologically active but still utilized in similar applications, often as a cost-effective alternative. Both types are derived from pantothenic acid (Vitamin B5) and are valued for their ability to enhance skin and hair health, though D-Panthenol is preferred for its higher efficacy.</p></p>
