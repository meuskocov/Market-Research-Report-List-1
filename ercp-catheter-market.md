<p><p>The ERCP catheter market is segmented into three main types: straight tip, tapered tip, and rounded tip catheters. Straight tip catheters enable precise access to the biliary tree, providing a direct approach for interventions. Tapered tip catheters facilitate easier navigation through complex anatomy, allowing smoother insertion and reduced trauma. Rounded tip catheters are designed for gentler contact with tissues, minimizing the risk of damage during procedures. Each type caters to specific procedural requirements, enhancing the efficacy of endoscopic retrograde cholangiopancreatography.</p></p>
