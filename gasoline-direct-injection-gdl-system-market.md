<p><p>Gasoline Direct Injection (GDI) systems enhance combustion efficiency in engines by precisely injecting fuel directly into the combustion chamber. The Turbulent Jet (T) system creates a swirling motion in the fuel-air mixture, improving atomization and combustion stability. Piezo fuel injectors use piezoelectric technology for rapid and accurate fuel delivery, optimizing performance and emissions. The Water Jet System integrates water injection to cool the intake charge, further increasing efficiency and reducing knocking. Together, these technologies advance engine performance and fuel economy.</p></p>
