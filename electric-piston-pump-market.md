<p><p>The electric piston pump market is categorized into two main types: 2-ball piston pumps and 4-ball piston pumps. The 2-ball piston pump features two balls that control fluid flow, offering simplicity and efficiency in low- to medium-pressure applications. In contrast, the 4-ball piston pump utilizes four balls, enhancing pressure management and flow control, making it suitable for high-pressure and demanding environments. Both types serve various industries, including oil and gas, automotive, and chemical processing.</p></p>
