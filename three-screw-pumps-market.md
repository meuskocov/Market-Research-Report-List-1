<p><p>Three screw pumps are positive displacement pumps commonly used for transporting a variety of fluids, including oils and viscous liquids. They consist of three intermeshing screws: one drive screw and two idler screws. This design allows for smooth and efficient operation, minimizing pulsation and ensuring steady flow, making them particularly suitable for applications in the oil and gas, marine, and industrial sectors.</p><p>The Three Screw Pumps Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 11.2% during the forecast period.
