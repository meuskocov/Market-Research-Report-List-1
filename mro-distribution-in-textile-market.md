<p><p>MRO (Maintenance, Repair, and Operations) distribution in the textile market encompasses various maintenance types. Preventive or scheduled maintenance focuses on regular, planned servicing to prevent equipment failure, ensuring smooth operations and minimizing downtime. In contrast, corrective maintenance involves reacting to equipment breakdowns, conducting repairs or replacements once issues arise. Both maintenance strategies are crucial for textile companies to maintain efficient production processes, reduce costs, and enhance equipment longevity, ultimately contributing to overall operational efficiency.</p></p>
