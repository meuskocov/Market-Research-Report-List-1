<p><strong>The Endoscope Reprocessors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Chamber</li><li>Multi Chamber</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The endoscope reprocessors market is categorized into two main types: single chamber and multi-chamber systems. Single chamber reprocessors typically offer a compact design, integrating all cleaning and disinfection processes in one unit, making them ideal for smaller facilities. In contrast, multi-chamber reprocessors consist of multiple compartments for different stages of the cleaning process, allowing higher throughput and efficiency for larger healthcare settings. Both types aim to ensure the proper sterilization of endoscopic instruments to maintain patient safety and infection control.</p></p>
